Our Story

About Us

Est. 2005

A Legacy of Sharp Looks

Dennis Best has been crafting sharp looks since 2005. What started as a one-chair shop is now one of the area's most trusted men's salons — known for precision cuts, classic shaves, and a welcoming atmosphere where every client feels like a regular.

Built on a foundation of craftsmanship and genuine hospitality, Dennis Best Men's Salon has grown by word of mouth, loyalty, and the simple belief that every man deserves to look and feel his best. From first haircuts to wedding-day grooming, we've been there for the moments that matter.

Our Journey

Shop Milestones

2005

The Beginning

Dennis Best opens a one-chair shop with a vision: deliver the finest cuts and classic barbershop experience to every client who walks through the door.

2010

Growing Reputation

Word spreads fast. The salon earns its first "Best Barbershop" recognition from local press, and a loyal client base begins to form around the Dennis Best name.

2012

Expansion

Growing demand leads to expanding the space, adding two additional barber chairs and bringing on Marcus Webb as senior barber — doubling capacity overnight.

2018

Premium Services

Launch of the signature Hot Towel Shave and Hair & Scalp Treatment services, cementing the salon's position as a full-service luxury men's grooming destination.

2020

Full Remodel

Complete interior renovation. The salon is reimagined with premium materials, custom cabinetry, and an elevated atmosphere that matches the quality of every cut.

2025

20 Years Strong

Celebrating two decades of sharp cuts, loyal clients, and a tradition of excellence. The Dennis Best legacy continues — one masterpiece at a time.
